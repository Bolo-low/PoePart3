History

The Exhibit A began in 2022 but a young artist named Alexa Kgare.

From her passion for art and music, a creative space was born for upcoming

artists, music enthusiast, art enthusiast and just genuine creatives like

herself to freely express their love for music and art. This gallery was her

way of sharing with the world what she hears, how she feels and what she

sees when listening to music or painting a mural. The Exhibit A is place

where people with different taste in art and music can come and connect

through their shared love for songs and art.




Vision and Mision Statement

Our mission is to unlock the world's creativity and their love
